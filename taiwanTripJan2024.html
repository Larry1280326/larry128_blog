BeiTou Hot Spring

Beitou Hot Spring facilities were developed while Taiwan was under Japanese Imperial Rule. At that time, the Japanese were very fond of hot springs and regarded them as a way of healing diseases. As time progressed, Beitou Hot Spring has developed into one of the popular leisure attractions in Taiwan.

Chiang Kai-Shek Memorial Hall

This memorial hall was built in memory of Chiang Kai-shek, the first president of the Republic of China. There is a ceremony for changing of the honored guards every hours.

National Dr. Sun Yat-sen Memorial Hall

This memorial hall was built in memory of Dr. Sun Yat-sen, the national father of Republic of China. Today, it mainly serves a platform for assembly, performance, conference and recreation.
